Who We Are

We are a Traditional Bosniak Folk dance group in Queens, New York. We do choregoraphies, folklor dances called "Kolo",such as Bosansko Kolo, Šota dance from Bosnia & Herzegovina, Sandžak, Plav & Gusinje, and Albania. We perform multiple choreographies ranging from (WIP)[Halaturka to Bosanske Igre]. We were originally organized in 1999, with 20 performs joining in the first year. Many people, hundreds, have passed through the doors of KUD Sedef and experienced folk dances from all around Bosnia, Sandzak, Gusinje, and other surrounding areas.
