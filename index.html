Who We Are

(WIP) We are a Traditional Bosniak Folk dance group in Queens, New York. We do choregoraphies, folklor dances called "Kolo",such as Bosanksko Kolo, Šota dance from Bosnia & Herzegovina, Sandžak, Plav & Gusinje, and Albania.
